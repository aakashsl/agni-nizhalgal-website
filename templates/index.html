About ANF

We the youngsters of Erode District get united to make our Erode much Greener. In the name of the Agni Nizhalgal Foundation, we are going to make our dreams come true.
We are proud to say that we are the followers of the vision and mission of our Nation Pride Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Sir.

Mission

Yellow City into Green City for its purest, make begger's free Society, Plant the Humanity as much as we can to the upcoming generation.Never go under others shadow ,create your own!

Vision

To ongoing sight of the society, be a changer and part of it. Greener and helper environment makes the life fullfilled at the end.Travelling through our path never fades for stranges on it.
