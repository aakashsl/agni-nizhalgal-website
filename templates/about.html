About Us

We the youngsters of Erode District get united to make our Erode much Greener. In the name of the Agni Nizhalgal Foundation, we are going to make our dreams come true.

We are proud to say that we are the followers of the vision and mission of our Nation Pride Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Sir.

So Agni Nizhalgal Foundation Inaugurated/started its journey on 27th July 2021 - Dr. APJ ABDUL KALAM SIR'S Memorial day.

This is the beginning of our journey with huge Support from many hearts. Interested volunteers can contact us at any time. We have an option for contribution. Interested volunteers can contribute with your name, place & contact no.

Our Motive

Greening the Environment as much as we can!

Agni Nizhalgal Foundation runs with youngsters and cannot be influenced by any other Foundations, Associations, Education Institutions, Companies, Third person and Political parties.

Our Foundation will support the Idea of every member which leads to Greening the Environment and public welfare and, It also gives first priority to Government areas and Hospital areas projects.
